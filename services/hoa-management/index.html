Frequently Asked Questions - HOA Management

What areas do you serve for HOA management?

We provide HOA management services throughout North Shore suburbs including Glenview, Northbrook, Highland Park, Lake Forest, Wilmette, Winnetka, Glencoe, Evanston, Buffalo Grove, Deerfield, and 20+ other suburban communities.

How do you handle covenant enforcement?

We provide professional covenant enforcement through regular community inspections, violation notices, architectural review coordination, and follow-up procedures while maintaining positive community relationships.

What makes your HOA management different?

We specialize in suburban planned communities with expertise in landscaping coordination, amenity management, community events, architectural guidelines, and maintaining property values through proactive community oversight.

What is included in your HOA management fees?

Our comprehensive HOA management includes board meeting support, covenant enforcement, architectural review coordination, vendor management, landscaping oversight, financial reporting, and resident communications. Transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

How do you coordinate landscaping and grounds maintenance?

We manage seasonal landscaping programs, coordinate with certified contractors, oversee irrigation systems, and ensure common areas maintain the high standards expected in North Shore communities.

Can you help organize community events?

Yes, we coordinate community events including seasonal celebrations, resident meetings, social activities, and special programs that build community spirit and enhance the neighborhood living experience.
