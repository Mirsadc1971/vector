Frequently Asked Questions - Condominium Management

What areas do you serve for condominium management?

We provide condominium management services throughout Chicago including Gold Coast, River North, Streeterville, Lincoln Park, and Lakeview, plus North Shore suburbs including Glenview, Highland Park, Evanston, Winnetka, and 30+ other communities.

What makes your condominium management different?

We specialize in high-rise and mid-rise condominium buildings with expertise in elevator systems, building automation, Illinois Condominium Property Act compliance, and complex building operations that condos require.

How long have you been managing condominiums?

Manage369 has been providing professional condominium management services in Chicago and North Shore for over 17 years, with CAI certification and deep expertise in Illinois condominium law.

What is included in your condominium management fees?

Our comprehensive management fees include board meeting facilitation, financial reporting, vendor coordination, emergency response, legal compliance monitoring, and unit owner communications. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

How do you handle elevator maintenance and emergencies?

We maintain relationships with certified elevator contractors and provide 24/7 emergency response for elevator issues. We coordinate regular inspections, preventive maintenance, and modernization projects while ensuring city compliance and resident safety.

Can you help with special assessments and reserve studies?

Yes, we coordinate professional reserve studies, help boards understand funding needs, and manage special assessment processes including board approvals, unit owner communications, and payment collection. Our goal is to maintain adequate reserves and avoid surprise assessments.
