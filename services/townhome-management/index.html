Frequently Asked Questions - Townhome Management

What areas do you serve for townhome management?

We provide townhome management services throughout North Shore suburbs including Hawthorn Woods, Long Grove, Kildeer, Lake Zurich, Lincolnshire, and other planned townhome developments in the Chicago area.

What makes townhome management different from condos or HOAs?

Townhome management requires specialized expertise in exterior building maintenance, shared utility coordination, roof systems, individual vs. common area responsibilities, and complex insurance coverage coordination unique to attached housing.

How do you handle shared utilities in townhome communities?

We coordinate shared water, sewer, and electrical systems, manage utility billing allocation, oversee maintenance of common utility infrastructure, and implement energy efficiency programs for townhome communities.

What is included in your townhome management services?

Our comprehensive services include exterior maintenance coordination, shared utility management, roof and gutter systems, driveway maintenance, insurance coordination, and common area management with transparent pricing.

How do you coordinate roof maintenance for townhome communities?

We manage regular roof inspections, coordinate repairs and replacements across multiple units, work with insurance companies for claims, and ensure compliance with building codes and manufacturer warranties.

Can you help clarify individual vs. common area responsibilities?

Yes, we develop clear responsibility matrices, coordinate with legal counsel when needed, and help establish policies that define individual unit owner responsibilities versus common area maintenance obligations.
