Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making our website and services accessible to all users.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Our Commitment to Accessibility

Manage369 is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

2. Accessibility Standards

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities.

Current Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen readers
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Alternative text for images
  • High contrast color schemes
  • Readable font sizes and spacing
  • Focus indicators for interactive elements
  • Descriptive link text
  • Form labels and instructions

3. Ongoing Improvements

We are actively working to improve accessibility across our website and services. Recent improvements include:

  • Enhanced keyboard navigation
  • Improved color contrast ratios
  • Better screen reader compatibility
  • Mobile accessibility enhancements
  • Form accessibility improvements

4. Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to work with assistive technologies including:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Voice recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Screen magnification software
  • Alternative input devices

5. Alternative Access Methods

If you encounter accessibility barriers on our website, we offer alternative ways to access our services:

  • Phone support: (847) 652-2338
  • Email assistance: service@manage369.com
  • In-person consultations
  • Alternative document formats
  • Verbal communication of website content

6. Feedback and Contact

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our website and services. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us.

Accessibility Feedback

When reporting accessibility issues, please include:

  • The specific page or feature affected
  • The assistive technology you are using
  • A description of the problem
  • Your contact information for follow-up

7. Third-Party Content

Some content on our website may be provided by third parties. While we strive to ensure all content meets accessibility standards, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party content.

8. Mobile Accessibility

Our website is designed to be accessible on mobile devices and tablets. We test our mobile accessibility regularly and make improvements as needed.

9. Document Accessibility

We strive to make all documents accessible, including PDFs and other downloadable content. If you need a document in an alternative format, please contact us.
