Transportation and Urban Connectivity

Lakeview's exceptional public transportation access makes it one of Chicago's most connected neighborhoods. The neighborhood's bike-friendly infrastructure, including proximity to the lakefront trail, makes cycling an attractive commuting and recreational option.
