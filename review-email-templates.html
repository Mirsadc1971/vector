📊 Review Request Best Practices

  • Timing: Request within 1-2 weeks of a positive interaction
  • Personalization: Always mention specific properties and achievements
  • Make it easy: Include direct links, don't make them search
  • Follow up once: If no response, follow up once after 2 weeks, then stop
  • Track requests: Keep a spreadsheet of who you've asked and when
  • Target: Aim for 2-3 review requests per month
  • Respond to all reviews: Reply within 24-48 hours to all reviews

Monthly Review Goals:

  • Google: 3-4 reviews per month (PRIORITY)
  • Facebook: 1-2 reviews per month
  • Yelp: 1 review per month
  • BBB: 1 review per quarter

Who to Ask:

  • ✅ Board presidents after successful meetings
  • ✅ Board members after completing projects
  • ✅ Long-term clients (1+ years)
  • ✅ Clients who've explicitly praised your service
  • ✅ After achieving significant cost savings
  • ❌ Don't ask unhappy clients
  • ❌ Don't ask during disputes or difficult negotiations
