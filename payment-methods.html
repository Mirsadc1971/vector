Online Payment Portal

Our secure owner portal provides comprehensive account management including payment processing, balance viewing, and document access. Your login credentials were provided with your welcome packet.

Making an Online Payment:

  1. Click the "Access Portal" button to visit our secure payment platform
  2. Enter your account credentials (username and password)
  3. Navigate to the "Payments" or "Make a Payment" section
  4. Select your payment method (credit card, debit card, or bank account)
  5. Enter payment amount and confirm transaction details
  6. Review and submit your payment for processing

Note: You'll receive email confirmation once your payment is successfully processed.

Bank Bill Pay Setup

Bank Bill Pay is the most economical way to manage your property management payments. Most major banks offer this service free of charge through their online banking platforms.

Step-by-Step Setup Instructions:

  1. Access Your Bank's Online Portal: Log into your bank's website or mobile app
  2. Find Bill Pay Service: Look for "Bill Pay," "Pay Bills," or "Payments" in the main menu
  3. Add New Payee: Click "Add Payee" or "Add New Bill" and enter:
    • Payee Name: Manage369
    • Address: 1400 Patriot Boulevard, Suite 357, Glenview, IL 60026
    • Account Number: Your property address or unit number
  4. Set Payment Amount: Enter your monthly assessment amount
  5. Schedule Payment: Choose one-time or recurring (recommended for monthly assessments)
  6. Include Reference Information: Add your property address and unit number in the memo field
Pro Tip: Set up recurring payments 5-7 days before your due date to ensure timely processing and avoid any late fees.

Payment FAQs

When are my monthly payments due?
Monthly assessments are typically due on the 1st of each month, with a grace period until the 5th (varies by association). Check your specific governing documents or welcome packet for your property's exact due dates and grace period details.
What fees apply to online payments?
Credit card and debit card payments incur a 3.5% processing fee. ACH/eCheck payments from your bank account are processed free of charge. We recommend using ACH payments or Bank Bill Pay to avoid fees.
What happens if my payment is late?
Late fees vary by association and are outlined in your governing documents. Typically, payments received after the grace period incur late fees ranging from $25-$75, plus potential interest charges. Chronic late payments may result in collection actions.
How can I check my account balance and payment history?
Log into your owner portal to view current balance, payment history, and upcoming charges. If you haven't received portal credentials or need assistance, contact our office at (847) 652-2338 for immediate setup.
Can I set up automatic recurring payments?
Yes! Both our online portal and Bank Bill Pay options support recurring payments. This ensures your assessments are always paid on time and helps you avoid late fees. We highly recommend this option for busy property owners.
What if I need to dispute a charge or have billing questions?
Contact our office immediately at (847) 652-2338 or email service@manage369.com. We'll review your account and work to resolve any billing discrepancies quickly. Documentation of the dispute will be maintained in your account file.
